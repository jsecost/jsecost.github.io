About Us

You are here: Home - About Us

Since 1991, thousands of children have learned to swim with the AquaSafe Swimming teaching method from our WSI and CPR-certified, experienced, enthusiastic instructors.

AquaSafe Swim School uses a customized technique to ensure that students get the most out of their swimming lessons. We're able to build an immediate rapport with each child by using games, songs, props and encouragement.

From the Founder

I was blessed to start AquaSafe Swimming in 1991 after graduating from UC Irvine and running a children's creative movement program which incorporated games, songs, props and fun as tools to teach dance. I applied this concept in the creation of AquaSafe Swimming by including interactive and entertaining techniques to develop a learn-to-swim program for babies and children (and adults too!).

For example, during a swim lesson, when we teach your child to flutter-kick using a kickboard, our teacher doesn't just say, "Kick across the pool," but rather, "Let's pretend you are a motor boat and have to get to the other side to rescue Mr. Frog." To teach side breathing, we talk to the fish with our bubbles... and listen to them by turning our head to the side and placing our ear on the water (while holding a toy underwater).

Our instructors are creative and energetic, and will design each swim lesson for your child in a fun and effective manner. That's my promise.
